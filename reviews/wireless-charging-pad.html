Features

So, what can you expect from a Wireless Charging Pad? Here are some of the key features to look out for: The latest wireless charging pads come with a range of features, including fast charging speeds, foreign object detection, and overcharge protection. Some pads also come with built-in cooling systems to prevent overheating, which can be a major concern for phone safety. Additionally, many wireless charging pads are designed with a compact and sleek design, making them a great addition to any home or office. When it comes to the technical specs, wireless charging pads typically operate at a frequency of 100-200 kHz and have a maximum power output of around 10-15 watts.
